Understanding Hantavirus

HPS (Pulmonary)

A severe respiratory disease found in the Americas, spread primarily by deer mice.

HFRS (Renal)

Common in Europe and Asia, affecting the kidneys and causing hemorrhagic fever.

Symptoms: When to Act

Early symptoms can be mistaken for the flu. Watch for these signs:

Early Stage:
  • Fever & Chills
  • Muscle Aches (Thighs/Hips)
  • Fatigue
Late Stage:
  • Shortness of breath
  • Coughing
  • Chest tightness
⚠️
Medical Warning: If you develop shortness of breath after being around rodent droppings, seek medical attention immediately.

Proven Prevention Strategies

  • Seal Up: Close holes in your home or garage to prevent rodent entry.
  • Trap Up: Use snap traps in areas where rodents are active.
  • Clean Up: Disinfect areas using a 10% bleach solution. Never sweep or vacuum!
