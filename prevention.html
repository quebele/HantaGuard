How to lower your risk of hantavirus infection.

This page shows the most important prevention steps: keep rodents out, avoid contaminated dust, and clean safely.

Spot symptoms early
Watch for fever, muscle aches, and breathing problems after possible rodent exposure.

Prevention

Keep rodents out

  • Seal cracks, gaps, and entry holes
  • Store food in sealed containers
  • Reduce clutter where rodents can nest
  • Use traps where appropriate

Clean safely

  • Ventilate enclosed areas first
  • Do not sweep dry droppings
  • Wear gloves and a mask when cleaning
  • Disinfect contaminated surfaces carefully

High-risk places

Risk is higher in enclosed spaces where rodents live or have lived, such as sheds, cabins, garages, attics, basements, barns, and storage rooms.

Good practice: Open and air out the area before you clean, then use wet cleaning and disinfecting methods instead of dry sweeping.
