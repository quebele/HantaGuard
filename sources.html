Tip 1
Spot symptoms early
Watch for fever, muscle aches, and breathing problems after possible rodent exposure.
Tip 2
Clean safely
Do not sweep dry droppings. Ventilate first and disinfect contaminated areas carefully.
Tip 3
Keep rodents out
Seal entry points, store food securely, and keep storage areas uncluttered.
